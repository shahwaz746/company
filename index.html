Graduate Tailor, a distinguished establishment in the world of bespoke tailoring, has been a beacon of sartorial excellence since its inception in 1991. Founded and meticulously nurtured by the dynamic duo of Kalam Uddin and Serfaraz, the atelier has become synonymous with craftsmanship and attention to detail. With a legacy spanning over three decades, Graduate Tailor has earned a stellar reputation for delivering impeccably tailored garments that seamlessly blend tradition with contemporary style. Kalam Uddin and Serfaraz, with their unwavering commitment to quality and customer satisfaction, have transformed the boutique into a thriving hub for individuals seeking personalized and elegant clothing. Their passion for the art of tailoring, coupled with a keen understanding of evolving fashion trends, has allowed Graduate Tailor to stand the test of time
